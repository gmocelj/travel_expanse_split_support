Privacy

Privacy Policy

Trevvy is designed to work without accounts and without uploading your trip data.

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Overview

Trevvy (“the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Data collection

Trevvy does not collect, store, or transmit your personal trip data to external servers. Trip data, expenses, and settings stay on your device.

What data is stored locally

The App may store the following information only on your device:

  • Trip names and details
  • Participant names
  • Expense records (amounts, categories, splits)
  • Settlement records
  • App preferences (theme, date format)

This data does not leave your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-party services

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data and device identifiers as described in their privacy policy.

RevenueCat privacy policy

Apple App Store

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. Apple’s privacy policy applies to transactions made through the App Store.

Data deletion

To permanently delete your data, uninstall the App. Because data is stored locally, there are no additional deletion steps required.

Advertising and tracking

Trevvy does not use advertising SDKs, tracking frameworks, or analytics services that collect personal data for marketing purposes.

Exchange rates

If you use multi-currency features, the App may fetch exchange rates from an external API. No personal trip data is sent with these requests.

Children’s privacy

Trevvy does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
